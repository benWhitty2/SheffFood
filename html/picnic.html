Picnic Locations

This is a list of the best picnic locations in and around the university campus.

The Student Union green space

The green space outside the student union is a lovely place to eat. On the grass or the benches you are close to nature and your classes. It is a great place to enjoy a pack-lunch or a meal from one of the student unions' many venues.

St George's Church

Although there is less seating than other options St George's Church is a beautiful building surrounded by greenery and mostly out of the way of traffic and pedestrians. All of this can make for a peaceful and scenic lunch right by the Diamond.

The Botanic Gardens

If you have some time on your hands and don't mind a short walk away from the campus, then there is no better option than the Botanic Gardens. The gardens are a huge park with almost unlimited places to sit and eat, all surrounded by nature, greenhouses filled with more tropical greenery, fountains, flower beads, and an old bear pit. There is much to explore in the botanic gardens. Not to mention the squirrels that are tame enough they will eat food right out of your hand.
